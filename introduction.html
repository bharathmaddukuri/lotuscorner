Introduction

To render coffee lovers a distinguished European style café experience, with lotus biscuit served with each coffee and lotus products present in most of the items in our coffee shop.

Each customer is served with the 3 Ls:

  • Lotus : Most of the items on our menu have a well balanced touch of lotus and it s products. The unique caramelized biscuit has caused a wave in the public taste and we are here to surf on it.
  • Love : A universal language that translates right from our ingredients through the Baristas to the drinks and food presented to all our customers.
  • Loyalty : We are loyal to our customers and our team. More than business, we aim to build relationships.

  • We see the rise of a world class café out of the prestigious Lotus Bakeries, Belgium. And the concept well translated to a larger audience across the world.
  • Offering high quality, standard products.
  • Developing a taste that will render to the local population.
  • Spread the Lotus Corner café experience worldwide.

  • We strongly promote charity work in Kuwait and it is our deepest desire to promote charity in all the locations we operate.
